BOLT DOCTOR

It is a Web based application that allows individuals to book appointments online through any web-connected device, such as a computer, laptop, smartphone or tablet. It lets the client to choose from different doctors, Specialty, how long is he going to wait and how much is he going to pay. Moreover, the system is integrated with third-party companies so that the customer can give feedback about his experience.
